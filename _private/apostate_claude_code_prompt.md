# Claude Code Prompt: The Apostate Deck Revisions

> **Note (2026-06-30):** The trial dates in this spec are superseded. On June 24, 2026 the Morphew trial was continued; it is now expected in the first half of 2027 with no court-set date yet. The "October 13 – November 20, 2026" range below (and any other trial-date references in this document) is historical. The live site `index.html` is the source of truth for current trial-date language.

## Context

This is the pitch deck for *The Apostate*, a documentary about poet Jason Morphew traveling alone to Alamosa, Colorado for the October 13 – November 20, 2026 murder trial of his cousin Barry Morphew. The deck is a single-scroll dark-themed site with red accent bars on section headers.

We are revising the deck to add an institutional accountability / social justice spine without losing Jason's interiority, which remains the emotional engine of the film.

## Preservation Principle (Read First)

**Do not weaken Jason's interiority anywhere.** THE PROTAGONIST, THE INHERITANCE (both THE NAME and THE SEDATIVE IN THE BONES), THE FORM, and THE TRADITION IT BELONGS TO are the heart of the film and must remain personal, poetic, and first-person. The new institutional material is a *frame* around Jason's journey, not a replacement for it. Where we add factual / institutional content, it should sit *next to* the interior material, not displace it.

The deck must continue to feel like it costs something to make.

## New Sections to Add

### NEW SECTION 1: Insert between THE WINDOW IS FIXED and THE HUMAN ARCHITECTURE

Title: **THE INSTITUTION ON TRIAL**

> **THE INSTITUTION ON TRIAL**
>
> This is not a typical murder trial. It is the second prosecution of the same man for the same crime — and nearly everyone who touched the first one paid for it.
>
> - **Second prosecution.** Barry has been arrested for this crime twice. The first case was dismissed in April 2022 on the eve of trial.
> - **A disbarred prosecutor.** DA Linda Stanley lost her law license in 2024 over her conduct in this case and others. The Colorado Supreme Court upheld disbarment in 2025.
> - **A judge who resigned.** Ramsey Lama left the bench in his early forties, citing health reasons, three months after dismissing the original case.
> - **Investigators off the case.** CBI agent Joseph Cahill resigned under a Brady letter from his own director. FBI agent Jonathan Grusing departed the Bureau. Chaffee County Sheriff John Spezze stepped down.
> - **A $15M civil suit, dismissed.** The Tenth Circuit upheld dismissal of Barry's federal civil rights lawsuit on April 6, 2026 — but its 129-page complaint is public record, and extraordinary.
> - **The forensic science is itself contested.** Of three labs consulted, only one detected BAM in Suzanne's femur. The defense will spend weeks attacking that lab's credibility.
> - **A defendant out on $3 million bond.** Rare for a Murder 1 charge. Funded through sources Barry's attorneys have declined to disclose.
> - **A courtroom under siege.** The trial court has restricted livestream access after true-crime channels violated prior orders.
>
> The system is trying to correct itself in real time. Jason is in the gallery while it does.
